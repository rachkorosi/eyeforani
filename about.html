About Us

Hello! We are a group of Cornell students who study the impact of technology on our surrounding ecosystems – both human and natural. Having played an integral role in the development of our generation, also known as “iGen", technology has special meaning to us. But, in many ways, it's also had a devasting impact on the environment around us and on the people involved in the production of devices we often use without second thought.

As an interdisciplinary team, we're focused on finding creative ways to educate the public about their technology consumption ... and about the ramifications that computing, especially regarding sourcing and extraction, has on a global scale.

Our Mission

Education

We believe that spreading awareness about the tribulations of sourcing and extraction, a process that is currently a cataclysmic part of the production of our devices, is the first step in creating tangible change.

Motivation

Our utlimate goal is simple: motivate people to act. We want to encourage users to be more mindful when making technology purchases, and to push for policy change that can improve the environmental and human impacts of sourcing and extraction. We believe that by coming together and speaking out, we can drive positive change and create a more just and equitable world.

Inspiration

We hope to inspire other people to take on their own projects when it comes to computing on earth. Look for wacky and wonderful ways to take action in your own lives to better your surrounding community. Nothing is too big or too small.
