water

The 100 L (26.4 gal) value for water is an estimate based on the water usage during the mining and manufacturing processes.

mining & manufacturing process

The 50 L (13.2 gal) value for water used during the mining process is based on the average water consumption for mining various metals. Additionally, the 50 L (13.2 gal) value for water used during the manufacturing process is based on the average water consumption for manufacturing electronic devices.

Sources:

[Columbia Water Center](https://water.columbia.edu/news-archive/global-water-consumption-mining-what-we-dont-know/) and [United Nations Industrial Development Organization - Water Use in Industries](https://www.unido.org/our-focus-advancing-economic-competitiveness-reduce-environmental-impacts-resources-efficient-and-cleaner-production).
