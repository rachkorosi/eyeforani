rare earth metals

The 3.5 lb (1.6 kg) value for other rare earth metals is based on the average content of rare earth metals in a smartphone and the amount of ore required to produce them.

Sources:

[USGS Mineral Commodity Summaries - Rare Earths](https://pubs.usgs.gov/periodicals/mcs2021/mcs2021-rare-earths.pdf) and [Statista - Rare earth mine production worldwide](https://www.statista.com/statistics/276325/rare-earth-production-by-country/).
