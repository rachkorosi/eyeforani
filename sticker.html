the smartphone sustainability nutrition label

Our smartphones have become an essential part of our daily lives, but the production process behind these devices has a hidden cost. To raise awareness about the environmental, social, and ethical impacts of smartphone production, we've created the Smartphone Sustainability Nutrition Label sticker. This unique sticker can be placed on the back of your phone, visually outlining the resources and methods involved in producing a smartphone and highlighting the need for change.

main features

Eye-catching design

The Smartphone Sustainability Nutrition Label sticker features a visually striking design that resembles a nutrition label, making it a conversation starter and an effective way to educate others about the unsustainability of smartphone production.

Informative content

The nutrition label includes detailed information about the materials, resources, and methods involved in producing a smartphone, such as mined ore, water consumption, greenhouse gas emissions, and labor conditions.

QR Code sticker option

For those who prefer a more discreet design or have limited space on their phone, we also offer a QR Code sticker that links to a webpage containing all the information from the nutrition label.

Support for NGOs

Proceeds from the purchase of the Smartphone Sustainability Nutrition Label sticker and QR Code sticker will be donated to NGOs working to address and mitigate the environmental and social issues related to smartphone production.
