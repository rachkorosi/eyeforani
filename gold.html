gold, silver, and platinum

The values for gold (0.2 oz or 5 g), silver (0.5 oz or 14 g), and platinum (0.02 oz or 0.5 g) are based on the average content of these precious metals in a smartphone and the amount of ore required to produce them.

Sources:

[[USGS Mineral Commodity Summaries - Gold](https://pubs.usgs.gov/periodicals/mcs2021/mcs2021-gold.pdf), [USGS Mineral Commodity Summaries - Silver](https://pubs.usgs.gov/periodicals/mcs2021/mcs2021-silver.pdf), and [USGS Mineral Commodity Summaries - Platinum](https://pubs.usgs.gov/periodicals/mcs2021/mcs2021-platinum-group-metals.pdf).
